About EuroClojure

EuroClojure is the largest Clojure conference in Europe and draws developers from all over the continent to gather and learn what is happening with the language, in the community, and within organizations using Clojure.


This year's event will be held in Bratislava, Slovakia from October 25-26. EuroClojure offers a great mix of experienced Clojurists and new adopters and everyone can find something to suit their needs.

getting there

By plane: Bratislava is served by two international airports: The M. R. Štefánik Airport, 9km from the city centre. Or Schwechat Airport (in Vienna), just 45 minutes to the Bratislava centre by bus or car.
By train: Bratislava's main railway station is a 10 minute walk from the city centre. Services from Austria, Germany, Hungary and more, terminate here.
By bus: Bus service from across Europe connects to Bratislava, with many services terminating at Bratislava's Central Bus Station at Mlynské nivy.

getting around

The venue is located in Old Town, with many attractions in walking distance. Bratislava has an extensive bus network with 60 routes by day and 18 at night. Additionally, 13 different tram lines service the city.
